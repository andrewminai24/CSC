The ultimate goal of this club is to help students succeed and excel in all their computer science related classes. We also hope to provide them with help and connections that will not only aid them in college, but also prepare them for a career in computer science.

Andrew Minai, President of M.C. C.S.C

Study Workshops

From intro to front-end and back-end development to STEM related courses, we offer study workshops on Tuesday for club members who need the help.

Collaborate With Peers

The Computer Science Club is your go-to community for all things computer science related. Whether it's through discord, in person, or on any of our social media platforms, we are the place where you can get help, form connections, or just have fun.

Campus Events

We are planning on having individuals who are experienced in the computer science industry give educational keynote presentations on campus. Our club also plans on having social events that will provide you with opportunities to meet new people and form connections.

Field Trips

Make sure to check out the upcoming-events tab on our Discord server for information regarding our planned events, including tours to technology companies, universities, and more.
